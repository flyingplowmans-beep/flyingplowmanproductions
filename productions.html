In November 2025, Flying Plowman Productions presented YOU by Mark Wilson in association with Coracle Arts at The Seven Dials Playhouse in London's Covent Garden as part of their 'Origins' season.

YOU is a powerful play about separation, longing and the invisible ties that bind us. Now in her mid 40s, Kathleen sits anxiously awaiting the arrival of the man she was forced to give up for adoption more than 30 years ago.

Cast: Sarita Plowman and James Dangerfield
Director: Rosie Snell
Original score composition: Andrew Stuart-Buttle
Movement consultant: Guillaume Piget
Show photography: Marshall Stay
Poster Photograph: Matt Jamie
Poster Design and Trailer/Marketing:George Wilson
Interview film and PR consultation: Flavia Fraser-Cannon
Set build and logistics: Greg Spong

As she finally allows herself to remember the events of that time, her story comes to life in a beautifully woven two-hander by BBC Radio writer Mark Wilson.

There are plans to tour this production to studio venues across the UK and Ireland — please let us know if your venue is interested.
